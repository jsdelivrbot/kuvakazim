KuvakaZim (Kuvaka means "Building" in Shona and "Asakheni" in Ndebele and "Zim" refers to Zimbabwe) is a platform to keep track of Zimbabwean parliamentarians. The platform promotes civic participation and openness for building a greater Zimbabwe.
